Weevrbird

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

Weevrbird is a personal information app for finite daily editions, Smartfeeds, saved context, and useful contributions. This policy explains what the current app stores, what it does not collect, and how to contact us.

Information Stored By The App

The current build may store the following information on your device:

  • Onboarding progress
  • Selected interests
  • Broad location preference, such as Global, United States, Nigeria, Atlanta, Lagos, London, Toronto, or Accra
  • Bird profile mark
  • Optional pen name
  • Email address when you start or complete the email-link identity flow
  • Saved pieces and opened reading history
  • Contribution drafts and submitted contributions
  • Profile setup and private connection count

Information We Do Not Collect In This Build

The current build does not request precise GPS location. It does not upload user photos, videos, or custom avatar images. It also does not provide production cross-device sync yet.

How Information Is Used

Weevrbird uses app state to:

  • Build a finite Today issue around selected interests and broad location
  • Keep saved pieces available in Library
  • Preserve contribution drafts
  • Show reading history and private connection count on your device
  • Prepare contribution and profile identity once email-link auth is connected

External Sources

Weevrbird may show attributed external articles, videos, public notices, and other source material as reading cards. External links may open outside Weevrbird. Those external sites have their own privacy practices.

Contributions

Contributions are saved privately first. You choose where a contribution belongs before it becomes visible in a Smartfeed. Production moderation, reporting, blocking, and backend storage still need to be implemented before a public launch.

Account Deletion And Local Data

The current build includes a copyable deletion request in Profile safety. Local device data can be cleared with Reset Weevrbird in Profile safety. Production account deletion should be implemented before public launch, or this policy should clearly describe the support process for deleting account data.

Contact

For privacy or support questions, email support@weevrbird.app.
