Weevrbird

Terms of Use

Last updated: July 19, 2026

Weevrbird helps people read finite daily editions, follow Smartfeeds, save useful pieces, and contribute notes, questions, recommendations, links, and long reads. These terms describe expected use of the app.

Accounts And Identity

The current build uses an email-link identity flow with an optional pen name. Users are responsible for using an email address they control and for keeping their device secure. Pen names should not impersonate another person, organization, or source.

Contributions

Contributions should:

  • Add useful context
  • Avoid spam, harassment, threats, hate, sexual exploitation, illegal instructions, or intentionally misleading claims
  • Respect other people's privacy
  • Avoid posting private information without permission
  • Include source context when sharing links, articles, images, videos, or resources

External Sources And Links

Weevrbird may show attributed external source cards and links. External links may open outside Weevrbird. Weevrbird does not control external websites, their content, or their privacy practices.

Moderation

Weevrbird may remove, limit, or refuse content that violates these terms or harms the usefulness and safety of the app. Production moderation, reporting, blocking, and appeal processes still need implementation before public launch.

User Content Rights

Users keep ownership of the content they create. By choosing to make a contribution visible in Weevrbird, users give Weevrbird permission to display, organize, and distribute that contribution inside the app. Users should only contribute content they have the right to share.

Local Data And Reset

The current build stores most app state locally on the device. Reset Weevrbird clears local setup, saved items, reading history, and contribution drafts on that device.

Disclaimers

Weevrbird is an information and discovery product. It should not be used as professional legal, medical, financial, safety, or emergency advice. Users should verify important information with original sources before acting on it.

Contact

For terms or support questions, email support@weevrbird.app.
