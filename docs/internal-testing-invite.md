## Slightly More Detailed Invite

Hey, I am opening up a small internal test for Weevrbird.

The idea: Weevrbird helps people keep up with articles, links, recommendations, and contributions without getting pulled into an endless feed. You choose interests and a broad location, then get a finite Today issue, Smartfeeds, a private Library, and simple contribution tools.

Install link:
`PASTE_TESTING_LINK_HERE`

A few things to try:

1. Complete onboarding.
2. Pick a bird profile mark and optional pen name.
3. Open a few Today pieces.
4. Save something to Library.
5. Paste an article, image, or video link into Contribute.
6. Check Profile and Safety.
7. Close and reopen the app to see if your state is still there.

Please tell me:

- Where did the app make sense right away?
- Where did you hesitate?
- Did anything feel too social, too vague, or not trustworthy?
- Did saving or contributing work the way you expected?
- Did anything look broken on your phone?

When reporting an issue, include your device, OS version, screen name, and a screenshot or screen recording if possible.
